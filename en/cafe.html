fika

(n.) a moment to slow down and appreciate the good things in life.
What I love most is sitting in a café, no matter the country or the weather. To chat, scribble, dream, work or simply let the hours pass. For me, the café goes beyond the cup. It’s a place, a refuge, a little world of its own. Sociologist Ray Oldenburg calls it a “third place,” a space somewhere between home and the office. The café is my third place. A corner to share time, ideas, a bit of oneself. I hope to go further and share here my secret addresses, favorite cafés, my little bubbles of sweetness around the world.
