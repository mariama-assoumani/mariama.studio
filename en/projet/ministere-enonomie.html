The Innovation Lab is part of the DSCI (Delegation for Synthesis, Coordination and Innovation) within the General Secretariat of the French Ministry of Economy and Finance. This multidisciplinary team brings together developers, data scientists, and a project manager to design and test innovative tools and services for Ministry agents.

Within the Lab, I contributed to several projects, including RéunioKit — a tool designed to help civil servants organize and facilitate collaborative workshops. I was responsible for user research: drafting interview guides, conducting user interviews and usability tests, and analyzing results through an Atomic Research approach. I also created reusable Figma components to improve design consistency.

Based on user feedback, I proposed and prioritized improvements in collaboration with the product team, while performing an accessibility audit to ensure compliance with RGAA standards. Additionally, I designed various visual supports, such as posters and activity reports, to enhance communication around the Lab’s initiatives.
