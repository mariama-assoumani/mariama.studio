Galerie MA+CH  

Target
Art and bubble tea lovers

Role
Marketing, Communication & Events

Year
2023

Galerie MA+CH is an art café-gallery located in Paris, known for its creative bubble teas and artistic exhibitions. This hybrid venue combines the enjoyment of handcrafted drinks with the discovery of emerging talents through exhibitions, workshops, and openings.

During my internship, I was responsible for creating, shooting, and editing content for Instagram (5,430 followers) and TikTok (1,738 followers), in order to enhance the gallery’s visibility and reach new audiences. I helped define a digital marketing strategy by identifying target audiences through data analysis and on-site observations.

I also contributed to the organization and promotion of events, ensuring consistency between online communication and on-site activities. For example, to support merchandise sales, I implemented an influencer marketing strategy with micro-influencers, accompanied by a dedicated launch event. This experience allowed me to strengthen my skills in content creation, community management, and event marketing.
