Pastry Application 

Client
Yann Couvreur

Role
UX/UI Design

Year
2024

Through the Google UX Design Certificate (200 hours), I deepened my expertise in UX research, interviews, user testing, prototyping, and human-centered design. I completed three end-to-end projects — from user needs analysis to interactive prototypes — including this pastry app for renowned French chef Yann Couvreur, celebrated for his delicate and poetic creations.

I applied the full design thinking process: conducting interviews, building empathy maps to capture pastry lovers’ needs, identifying pain points, ideating solutions through mind mapping, and structuring information architecture. I then designed low- and high-fidelity wireframes, prototypes, and ran usability tests to deliver a refined, user-centered experience. Accessibility was a key focus — I annotated each screen according to WCAG standards, ensuring sufficient contrast, legible typography, and intuitive navigation.
