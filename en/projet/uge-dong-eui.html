Interactive Documentary 

Client
UGE & Dong-Eui

Role
Development & UX/UI Design

Year
2025

As part of my Master’s in Cultures and Web Professions (Université Gustave Eiffel) in partnership with Dong-Eui University in Busan, I had the opportunity to spend one month in South Korea to co-create an interactive documentary and its dedicated website.

Alongside 14 French and Korean students, we explored trot (트로트) — a Korean music genre with deep cultural roots, often considered old-fashioned but now enjoying a revival.

As the project’s web developer, I oversaw digital art direction, UX/UI design, mockup creation, and front-end integration using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My work included user research, multilingual interface design, and implementing a light/dark mode to enhance the overall experience.

This 100% hand-coded project (no CMS) offered complete creative freedom and technical optimization: lightweight performance, fast loading, SEO-friendly, and fully responsive. It strengthened my skills in project management, cross-cultural teamwork, and technical autonomy — leading to a unique and efficient final product.
