As part of the Marketing Department of Dacia France, I joined the Digital & Marcom Service as an Assistant Digital Project Manager. I contributed to the management and optimization of the dacia.fr website, which attracts more than two million visitors per month. I also managed the Dacia Community Forum, which gathers over 130,000 members, and helped generate leads by ensuring consistency between content, platforms, and customer needs.

I took part in defining and tracking the publishing schedule: product plans, dedicated French pages, reveal campaigns, carousels, banners, and commercial offers. I wrote update briefs, coordinated their deployment with internal teams, and ensured deadlines were met in collaboration with product and advertising managers.

I also performed weekly website monitoring to identify and correct potential issues, while conducting competitor benchmarks. To improve SEO performance, I optimized pages following specific recommendations. I used the AEM (Adobe Experience Manager) CMS to independently integrate and update web content. Lastly, I participated in the creation of mockups to enhance specific pages and improve the overall user experience across online journeys.
